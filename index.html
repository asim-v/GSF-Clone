    Google Science Fair

    If you’re 13-18 years old, we’d love for you to join us in sharing your best ideas with the world.

    It’s easy to get started

    You can go solo or grab some friends.

    1. Find a problem you’d like to solve.

    2. Think up ideas and select the best one.

    3. Explore your solution by testing it out.

    4. Share your results.
